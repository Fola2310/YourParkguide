YourParkguide is a simple tool that helps car owners remember exactly where they parked and guides them back to their car without stress. Oftentimes, many people forget their parking location at large events, malls, airports or churches. This leads to frustration, time wasted and sometimes panic. The feature to solve this already exists inside Google Maps, but most people do not know about it. YourParkGuide makes it visible, easy to use and always accessible.

Why It Matters

Losing track of where you parked is more common than people admit. With crowded parking areas and distractions, the memory of “I parked here” fades. YourParkGuide reduces confusion and helps users stay in control, calm and confident when returning to their cars.

Who It’s For

 Everyday car owners
People who attend large programs or events
Travelers at airports or motor parks
Church and stadium event attendees
Fleet and delivery drivers

Key Features

One-Click Save: Automatically records your parking location when you stop driving.
Guided Return: Uses map or AR directions to take you back to your car.
Smart Reminders: Alerts you if your parking is time-limited or at night.
Parking History: View past parking spots if needed.
Share Location: Send your car location to friends or family.
Smart Alert: Security notification if suspicious movement is detected.


Technology Used

YourParkGuide is powered by:

GPS location services
Google Maps API
ARCore (for augmented reality guidance)
Mobile app frontend (e.g., React Native or Flutter)
